Books in Progress

 

Krusche, K.                Rediscovering the Roman Forum, Ruins of architectural edifices and what do they tell us?

(in preparation).          This book is collection of the findings from analysis of the Roman Forum measure drawings and archival research associated to it. 

Krusche, K.                 Transformation of Historic Urban Mumbai from 1600’s till Today

(in preparation).          Documenting and constructing the history of the city of Mumbai through cartography and archival sources. 

Krupali, K.                  Rediscovering the Mughal Tombs and Gardens

(in preparation).          Outlines the work done to document the waterfront of Agra and how the tombs and gardens in the Mughal period where built, including Taj Mahal and 46 tombs and garden on the site.
