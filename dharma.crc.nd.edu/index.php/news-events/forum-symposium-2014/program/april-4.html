The Digital Future of World Heritage

The University of Notre Dame Rome Global Gateway
Via Ostilia 15, 00184 Rome

Friday, 4 April 2014
09:00 Gathering for coffee
  Location: University of Notre Dame Rome Global Gateway, Via Ostilia 15
   
09:30 Heritage Management Workshop
  Hosted by John Stubbs, Senior Professor of Architectural Preservation Practice and Director of the Master of Preservation Studies program, School of Architecture, Tulane University
  Panel includes:
  Introduction by John Stubbs
  9:40 Veronique Dauge, Head of Culture Unit - UNESCO Regional Bureau for Science and Culture in Europe
  10:00 Patrizia Fortini, Soprintendenza Speciale per i Beni Archeologici di Roma
  10:20 Lisa Ackerman, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, WMF
  10:40 Robin F. Rhodes, Department of Art, Art History, and Design, Department of Classics, School of Architecture, University of Notre Dame
  11:00 DISCUSSION Best practice of management and scholarship for advancement of heritage sites
   
12:00 Casual luncheon at Notre Dame Rome Centre
   
13:30 NASA Workshop: “New Directions in World Heritage Scholarship”
  Hosted by Jarek Nabrzyski and Krupali Krusche, focusing on innovations by scholars in the field for new research in advancing the field
  NASA Panelist talks:
  13:30 Jarek Nabrzyski, Director, Center for Research Computing, University of Notre Dame
  13:50 Compton J. Tucker, Hydrospheric and Biospheric, NASA
  14:10 Thomas L. Sever, Marshall Space Flight Center, NASA
  14:30 Douglas C. Comer, Principal, Cultural Site Research and Management, Inc. (CSRM)
  14:50 Ronald Blom, Program Manager, Solid Earth Science and Natural Hazards, NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory
  15:10 DISCUSSION Best practice of technology for advancement of heritage sites
   
16:30 Break with refreshments
   
16:45 CLOSING PANEL hosted by Dennis Doordan, Associate Dean of Research, Scholarship and Creative Work, School of Architecture, University of Notre Dame
   
17:45 SYMPOSIUM ADJOURNMENT by Krupali Krusche, Director of DHARMA (Digital Historical Architectural Research and Material Analysis), University of Notre Dame
   
18:15 RECEPTION hosted by the School of Architecture, University of Notre Dame with remarks by Dean Michael Lykoudis
  Location: La Terrazza at the Notre Dame Rome Global Gateway, Via Ostilia 15
20:45 (Optional) Walking Tour: Baroque Rome

REGISTRATION CLOSED
